About the Artist

Welcome to my artistic universe! I'm Pepe, a passionate illustrator creating diverse furry art with all kinds of characters and themes.

While I work with a wide variety of subjects and styles, my highlight and specialty is historical military themes, particularly World War I and World War II. From detailed military uniforms and authentic weaponry to atmospheric scenes and period-accurate vehicles, I love bringing history to life through anthropomorphic art.

Whether you're looking for a character icon, a detailed full-body illustration with backgrounds, or a complex scene, I work in both traditional and digital mediums to bring your vision to reality.
